UNLEASHED CLO.

The brand began its journey in November 2024 by selling and marketing products online, primarily through social media platforms and other digital channels to reach its target market. Through consistent promotion, creative branding, and customer engagement, the brand gradually gained recognition and attracted a growing customer base.


As the demand for its products increased, the business expanded its operations and successfully established its own physical store. This transition marked a significant milestone, enabling the brand to strengthen its identity, provide a direct shopping experience for customers, and showcase its products in a dedicated retail space.


At present, the brand continues to grow, combining its online presence with its physical store operations to reach a wider audience and cater to the needs of its expanding market.

BRAND MISSION

Our mission is to deliver high-quality and stylish clothing that reflects confidence, comfort, and individuality. We aim to connect with our customers through innovation, authenticity, and exceptional service. By embracing modern fashion trends and sustainable practices, we strive to build a community that celebrates self-expression and personal style.

BRAND VISION

To become a leading local clothing brand that inspires confidence and creativity in every Filipino, recognized for its originality, customer-centered approach, and strong presence in both digital and physical fashion spaces.
